# FaceCam Mobile App Guide

This guide provides instructions on how to set up and use the dedicated FaceCam mobile application for tournament broadcasting.

## 1. Download & Installation

The FaceCam app is currently available as a direct APK download for Android devices.

*   **Download Link**: [Download Ecube_FaceCam.apk](https://github.com/al-mohaimin-farabi/facecam2.0-App/releases/download/v1.0.0/Ecube_FaceCam.apk)
*   **Install Instructions**: 
    1.  Open the downloaded `.apk` file.
    2.  If prompted, allow your browser or file manager to "Install apps from unknown sources".
    3.  Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation.

---

## 2. First Launch & Permissions

When you open the app for the first time, it is **critical** that you grant all requested permissions.

*   **Camera Access**: Required to capture your video feed.
*   **Microphone Access**: Required for mandatory tournament audio.
*   **Storage Access**: May be requested for configuration files.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you deny any permissions, the app will not be able to stream properly. You can reset permissions in your phone's **App Settings** if you accidentally declined them.

---

## 3. How to Connect

1.  Open the **FaceCam App**.
2.  Enter your unique **Stream Key** (provided by your tournament administrator).
3.  Tap **Connect** or **Start Broadcast**.
4.  Verify that your video appears on the screen and the audio indicator (green bar) is moving when you speak.

---

## 4. Mandatory Settings

To ensure a professional and uninterrupted broadcast, please follow these rules:

*   **Device Orientation**: The FaceCam app supports both **landscape and vertical (portrait)** orientations. You can position your device however is most comfortable for you to get the best angle.
*   **Do Not Disturb (DND)**: Enable DND mode to block incoming calls and notifications. A single phone call can cut your camera feed during a match.
*   **Stay Plugged In**: Video streaming consumes significant battery. Keep your device connected to a power source throughout the match.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, please try these steps:

*   **Black Screen / Refusing to Connect**: Completely close the app (swipe it away from your recent apps) and reopen it.
*   **Audio Issues**: Ensure your microphone isn't physically covered and that permissions are enabled.
*   **Poor Connection**: Move closer to your Wi-Fi router. Wired connections via USB-C to Ethernet adapters are highly recommended for the best stability.
